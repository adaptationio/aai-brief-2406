# 7-Day Sprint — from zero to first dollar

One week. The aim is **cash booked**, not a perfect business. Move fast, fix later.

Each day is ~1–2 focused hours. Check the boxes.

---

## Day 1 — Pick the lane & arm yourself
- [ ] Pick **one ICP** from `OFFER.md` (start with your warmest network segment).
- [ ] Pick **one "hero" automation** you can build with Claude in <2 hours
      (proposal drafter / inbox triager / report-or-brief generator / data cleaner).
- [ ] **Build it once for a fake/sample client** so you have a live demo. This is your
      whole sales pitch — the working thing beats any deck.
- [ ] Set up the boring rails: a calendar link, an invoice method (Stripe/Wise/PayPal),
      a one-line bio.

## Day 2 — Build the list
- [ ] Write down **20 warm contacts** (rows 1–2 of `OUTREACH.md`): past calls, inbound
      that ghosted, network, newsletter readers.
- [ ] Add **20 niche-cold** names in your chosen vertical.
- [ ] Make a 3-column tracker: Name | Last touch | Status. A spreadsheet is fine.

## Day 3 — Send (warm)
- [ ] Send **10 warm** messages (personalized — fill the brackets for real).
- [ ] Record a **2-min Loom** of your hero automation for the value-first DMs.
- [ ] Respond to anything that comes back **within the hour**.

## Day 4 — Send (more) + first calls
- [ ] Send **10 more** touches (finish warm, start niche-cold).
- [ ] Take any booked call. Run the 20-min structure from `OUTREACH.md`.
- [ ] **Show Claude live on the call.** Do their task in front of them.

## Day 5 — Close + deliver
- [ ] Send proposals/invoices to everyone who leaned in. **Same-day, while warm.**
- [ ] For anyone closed: **deliver the real build** with Claude, hand it over, demo it.
- [ ] Ask the offer-converting question: "$500 one-off, or $250 now + first month of
      Monthly free?"

## Day 6 — Follow up (the money day)
- [ ] **+3 day nudge** to everyone who went quiet (template in `OUTREACH.md`).
- [ ] Convert at least one one-off into the **Monthly retainer** — that's the recurring
      $200 locked in.
- [ ] Ask every happy contact for **one referral**.

## Day 7 — Bank it & systematize
- [ ] Confirm the **definition of done** (see `README.md`): a paid one-off ≥$200, OR a
      retainer ≥$200/mo, OR ≥3 proposals out with verbal yes.
- [ ] Write down what landed and what flopped. **Resell the winning automation** —
      same build, next client, is how $200/mo becomes real money.
- [ ] Re-arm: refill the list to 20 warm + 20 cold for next week.

---

## If you hit a wall

| Stuck on | Do this |
|---|---|
| No replies | Your message is too long or too "salesy." Cut it in half. Lead with the artifact (Loom), not the offer. |
| Calls but no close | You're telling, not showing. Do their task live in Claude on the call. |
| Closes but it feels too cheap | Good — raise the anchor $100 next client. The first one buys the testimonial. |
| Can't find 20 warm names | You have more than you think: email "sent" folder, LinkedIn connections, anyone who ever asked "can you help with X?" |

---

## The one number that matters

Track **booked revenue**, nothing else, daily. Vanity metrics (sends, opens, likes)
don't pay for Max. The sprint succeeds the moment one line item is **paid or agreed**.
